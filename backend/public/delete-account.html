Delete Your Account

Permanently remove your NovaEdge account and all associated data.

⚠️

This action is permanent and cannot be undone. Please read the information below carefully before proceeding.

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed from our servers:

  • Your profile — name, email, and account credentials
  • Tool usage history — all records of your tool usage and activity
  • Subscription data — your plan details and billing records
  • Push notification tokens — FCM device registration
  • Any files processed — images, QR codes, invoices temporarily stored

Before You Delete

Please ensure you have:

  • Downloaded any invoices or files you want to keep
  • Cancelled your active subscription (if any) to avoid future charges
  • Saved any data you need from the App

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your account in two ways:

1

From the App: Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account and confirm with your password.

2

From this page: Use the form below to request account deletion by providing your registered email and password.
